Top Performing Areas
SDGs with highest scores
SDG 9 – Industry & Innovation (3.8): Core business strongly aligned with innovation and digital infrastructure — Google's highest-scoring SDG.
SDG 7 – Affordable & Clean Energy (3.5): Global leadership in renewable energy procurement with 24/7 Carbon-Free Energy ambition.
SDG 17 – Partnerships (3.0): Extensive collaborations with NGOs, governments, and suppliers globally including COP29 and UNGA participation.
SDG 12 – Responsible Consumption (2.8): Circular economy initiatives with 8.8M data center components reused and 82% zero-waste manufacturing.

Priority Gaps
Areas requiring urgent attention
SDG 10 – Reduced Inequalities (0.5): Limited systemic reach beyond accessibility features; significant gap in addressing structural inequalities.
SDG 2 – Zero Hunger (0.8): Minimal direct impact; only indirect support through AI and data tools for agricultural use cases.
Scope 3 Emissions: Increased 22% YoY due to data center construction and hardware manufacturing — critical challenge for net-zero goals.
Asia-Pacific Renewables: Renewable energy adoption among suppliers progressing slower in APAC due to grid constraints.
